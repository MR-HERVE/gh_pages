short biography of the group

H_art the band consist of three artists;Mordecai Mwinyi who is the lead singer, Wachira Gatama who is the poet in the group and Kenneth Muya who is the guitarist. The group before used to perform play,excluding Mordecai who joined them later.Their journey was not a walk in the park for they sometimes had to sleep while travelling especially when they had to perform far places with minimum wages. They sang a political song during an election period in 2013,where people asked the bands`name but they had not figured that out.Their first interview was done by Caroline Mutoko from kiss Fm of their songs "uliza kiatu"was a bit hit at the time.
